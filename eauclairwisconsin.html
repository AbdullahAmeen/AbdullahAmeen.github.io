Eau Claire County of Wisconsin


According to wikipedia: Eau Claire is the singular form of the original French name, Eaux Claires, meaning Clear Waters. The area that Eau Claire was settled on was first discovered in the late 19th century. French fur traders began settling there for work.

In the Summer 2019 a group of high school students from the Upward Bound program and I traveled to Eau Claire County of Wisconsin. We stayed for a four weeks summer program at the Universtiy of Wisconsin Eau Claire.

During our stay in Eau Claire Wisconsin, we have visited several historical and educational places/institutions. Besides some academics programs at the universtiy of Wisconsin Eau Claire, we had fun and really enjoyed our stay by visiting some popular places such as parks, musums, colleges, water parks, Zoo and more.
