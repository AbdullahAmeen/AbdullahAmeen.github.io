About me


Hello! I am Abdullah Ameen and I just completed my master degree in GIS and Cartography from the University of Wisconsin Madison.

In 2001, I graduated with my bachelors degree in Civil engineering from Kabul University-Afghanistan. During my junior and senior years at the university I taught mathematics courses to high school students, which I enjoyed greatly.

I am a Civil engineer by profession and have over 12 years of diverse experiences working with International organizations and USAID contractors in Community Development/Subnational Governance, Project Management, Water Supply networks design, GIS Analysis, Education and Social Services.

I speak three different languages Pashto, Persian/Dari and English. I also understand the dialects of Hindi/Urdu and basic Arabic languages. For more details, please see my resume.

In 2014 my family and I immigrated to the United States of America and just recently we became United States Citizens.

I am passionate about the art and science of writing code, making maps, visualizing/analyzing data, teaching mathematics, developing static websites, solving problems and last but not least, helping others.
